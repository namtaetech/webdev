Andrew Ross Sorkin, auther of Too Big to Fail

Feburary 2017

New York Tomes columnist and author, Andrew Ross Sorkin, has been described as "the most famous financial journalist of his generation. His Book, Too big to fail, was the first behind-the-sences account of the financial crisis that lead to our current ressesion.

The Economist, The financial Times, and Business Week all named Too Big To fail one of the best book of the year. The Book was published by the Viking October 20,2009. The Book was adapted as a movie by HBO Films and premiered on HBO on May 23, 2011.
